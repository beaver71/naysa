About Us

India is a country of well over a billion people. With experts predicting India to become a formidable market for Internet and Mobile Users by 2017, Content Providers are now looking at India as a billion-dollar venture. The Indian Telcos, catering to a people of multiple billion, are constantly searching for fresh content to keep their subscribers engaged.


Naysa Vas Communications is a company founded on the principles of providing content solution for the mobile and internet service providers while delivering a safe passage for the International Content Providers, creating a lucrative opportunity for them and the Indian Telcos. Our primary focus is to maximize revenue generation for both parties- the Content Providers and the Indian Telco Giants
